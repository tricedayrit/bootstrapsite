HOMEPAGE

Welcome to my website! I am Beatrice Dayrit: college student, artist, barista, and writer.

Originally from Bend, Oregon, I moved to Idaho to attend Boise State under the GIMM program. As of April 2020, my projected graduation date is in May 2022.

Through college, I've been working at Dutch Brothers, modeling, painting, and woodburning for fun and to earn some extra cash. Through the Great Quarantine of 2020, I took up woodworking and sculpting.

I have a little brother whom I adore. In my spare time, I enjoy hiking, cooking, and swimming. I've also enjoyed coding since high school, and have been working with bootstrap since I was fourteen.
If I'm not in the mountains or in a river, I'm usually in front of my computer writing. I'm currently working for fun on a textbook-style guide to Skyrim trivia. I also enjoy writing works of fiction as well as posting to forums about Star Wars and MCU (Marvel Cinematic Universe) discourse.
